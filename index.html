Photography and Coding

Can photography and coding complement each other?

Photography and coding may seem like distinct fields, but they complement each other in powerful ways, especially when it comes to telling visual stories. Both require creativity, attention to detail, and problem-solving, but what really connects them is their power to create narratives. With photography, I capture moments that convey emotion and meaning, while coding lets me build interactive experiences that bring those visuals to life.

About me

As an amateur photographer, I love capturing the world around me, and I’m currently learning programming to blend creativity with tech. I’m a lifelong learner with a passion for nature, which led me to study biology, complete a master's in environmental and sustainability sciences, and earn a second degree in economics. I’m always excited to learn and take on new challenges.

About this website

This website is created as a way to document my journey into coding while sharing my photography work. It’s a space where I combine my passion for visual storytelling with my exploration of programming. As I learn new coding skills, this site will evolve alongside my work, reflecting both my creative growth and technical development.
